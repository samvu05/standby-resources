# Privacy Policy for StandBy – Desk & Night Clock

**Last updated: 2025-12-11**

## 1. Introduction
StandBy – Desk & Night Clock (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy.  
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the App.

## 2. Information We Collect
We **do not collect, store, process, or share any personal or non-personal data**.  
The App does not access, transmit, or require any information from your device.

## 3. Offline Operation
The App works **entirely offline** and **does not require an internet connection**.  
No data is sent to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

## 4. Permissions
The App only uses minimal system functionalities necessary for its features.  
No permissions are used for collecting or transmitting user data.

## 5. Third-Party Services
The App does **not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertisements, or tracking technologies**.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
Since the App does not collect any data, it is safe for users of all ages, including children.
